Run GPS in background

By default when phone screen goes off then GPS2Bluetooth will stop receiving GPS signal.

Settings to run app in background:

Background in Android 15

Settings > Battery > Power saving > Off

Settings > Apps > GPS2Bluetooth > Battery > Unrestricted

After screen goes off, GPS2Bluetooth should keep receiving GPS signal in the background.
